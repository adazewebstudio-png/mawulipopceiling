Classic POP Archway in Kasoa

Smooth Circular Arch Transition

For this newly built family home in Kasoa, the owner wanted to soften the transition between the main hall and the corridor leading to the bedrooms. We replaced the standard rectangular lintel with a smooth, semi-circular POP arch. The curve was hand-leveled and finished with fine skimming compound to ensure a perfect arc. The new arch adds a welcoming and stylish feel to the home's interior.

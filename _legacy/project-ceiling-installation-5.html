Grand Hall Ornate Design

Classical Luxury & Ambience

This grand project for a mansion in Ajiringanor showcases classical POP artistry at its finest. The centerpiece is a large, hand-carved ornate flower medallion that serves as the anchor for a crystal chandelier. Surrounding this are deep recessed tray borders that hide warm LED cove lighting, casting an elegant golden glow across the entire hall. The combination of traditional craftsmanship and modern lighting creates a majestic atmosphere, perfect for a premium Ghanaian home. Every detail, from the stepped edges to the intricate floral patterns, was meticulously finished by hand.

Service: Ceiling Installation
Location: Ajiringanor, Accra
Time Taken: 10 Days
